Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 April 2026

TL;DR

PrivMark is a desktop app that runs entirely on your machine. We don't collect telemetry, analytics, or crash reports. The app only contacts the network in two situations: when you activate your license (once), and when it checks for updates (which you can disable). Your documents never leave your computer.

Who we are

Wolfberry AB, a Swedish limited company, is the data controller for PrivMark. You can reach us at hello@privmark.app.

What we collect and why

License activation (once, at first launch)

When you activate your license, PrivMark sends your license key and a randomly generated machine identifier to our payment processor, LemonSqueezy, to verify the license is valid and has not exceeded its activation limit. LemonSqueezy returns an activation token which is stored locally on your device. We never receive or store your license key on our own servers.

  • Legal basis: contract performance (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))
  • Recipient: Lemon Squeezy, LLC (Merchant of Record)
  • Transmitted: license key, machine identifier
  • Not transmitted: your name, email, documents, or usage

Update checks (optional, on launch)

If automatic updates are enabled (the default), PrivMark fetches latest.yml from GitHub Releases approximately 3 seconds after launch to see if a newer version is available. GitHub receives your IP address and user-agent as part of this standard HTTP request. You can disable this at any time in Settings → Updates, or permanently by launching the app with --no-auto-updates.

  • Legal basis: legitimate interest in providing security updates (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))
  • Recipient: GitHub, Inc.
  • Transmitted: none from our side; GitHub logs standard request metadata

Purchase (if you buy a license)

When you purchase PrivMark, the transaction is handled by LemonSqueezy, which acts as the Merchant of Record. LemonSqueezy collects the personal data required to process payment and issue invoices (name, email, billing address, VAT number if applicable). We receive only the information needed to issue and track your license: your email and order ID. For LemonSqueezy's own privacy practices, see lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

What we don't do

  • No telemetry, usage analytics, or feature tracking
  • No crash reporting (no Sentry, no Bugsnag, no Google Crashpad)
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies (the app is not a web app; the landing page uses none)
  • No document content leaves your device — ever
  • No account required to use the app

Data stored on your device

PrivMark stores the following files locally, in your operating system's user data directory:

  • settings.json — your preferences
  • window-state.json — last window size and position
  • license.json — activation token from LemonSqueezy (if licensed)
  • trial.json — trial start date (if in trial)

These files never leave your device. Deleting the app removes them.

Retention

We retain purchase records (email, order ID) for as long as required by Swedish bookkeeping law (7 years). License keys remain valid indefinitely — we do not expire keys after refund windows.

Your rights under GDPR

If you live in the European Economic Area, Switzerland, or the United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (subject to our bookkeeping retention obligations)
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (in Sweden: Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@privmark.app.

International transfers

LemonSqueezy and GitHub are US-based companies. Personal data may be processed in the United States under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page and — for existing customers — notify you via email to the address associated with your purchase.
